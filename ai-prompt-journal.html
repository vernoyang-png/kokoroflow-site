ChatGPT Prompts for Creators

How to use this page

Pick one prompt, add your audience and topic, then ask ChatGPT for a first draft. Review the result, check facts, adjust the voice, and save the best prompt in your own journal.

1. ChooseSelect a prompt for the task.
2. Add contextInclude audience, goal, tone, and format.
3. ReviewEdit facts, examples, and brand voice.
4. ReuseSave what worked for next time.

Starter prompt list

Content ideasGive me 20 content ideas for [audience] who want to [goal]. Group them by beginner, intermediate, and advanced.
Blog outlineCreate an SEO-friendly outline for a blog post about [topic] for [audience]. Include H2 sections and search intent.
Pinterest pin titlesWrite 15 Pinterest-friendly titles for a pin about [topic]. Keep them clear, benefit-driven, and under 70 characters.
Short video scriptWrite a 30-second short video script teaching [topic]. Include a hook, 3 points, and a simple call to action.
Email newsletterDraft a short newsletter for [audience] about [topic]. Make it helpful, calm, and practical.
Product ideaSuggest 10 simple digital product ideas for [audience] based on problems related to [topic].
Freelance offerHelp me turn my skill in [skill] into 5 clear freelance service packages for [target customer].
Website copyWrite homepage copy for a website that helps [audience] solve [problem]. Include headline, subheadline, and CTA.
Repurpose contentTurn this blog idea into 5 Pinterest pins, 3 short videos, and 1 newsletter topic: [idea].
Weekly planCreate a simple 7-day content plan for [audience] around [topic], using one main idea per day.
Audience researchDescribe the main goals, fears, questions, and buying triggers of [audience] interested in [topic].
Problem listList 25 practical problems [audience] faces when trying to [goal]. Rank them by urgency and content potential.
Lead magnetCreate 10 free download ideas for [audience] about [topic]. Include title, promise, and what pages it should contain.
Product descriptionWrite a clear product description for [product] for [audience]. Focus on benefits, use cases, and simple outcomes.
FAQ builderCreate 12 frequently asked questions and answers for beginners learning about [topic].
Comparison articleCreate a comparison outline for [tool/product A] vs [tool/product B] for [audience]. Include who each one is best for.
Affiliate disclosureWrite a short, friendly affiliate disclosure for a blog post recommending [product type]. Keep it transparent and simple.
Social captionsWrite 10 Instagram or Threads captions about [topic] for [audience]. Keep them practical and non-hype.
YouTube outlineCreate a YouTube video outline about [topic] with hook, intro, 5 sections, examples, and CTA.
ChecklistCreate a beginner-friendly checklist for [audience] who want to complete [task].
Landing page structureDesign a simple landing page structure for a free [resource] about [topic]. Include sections and CTA text.
SEO keywordsSuggest keyword ideas for [topic], grouped by informational, commercial, and beginner search intent.
Content calendarBuild a 30-day content calendar for [audience] around [topic], including blog, Pinterest, and email ideas.
Rewrite for clarityRewrite this text to be clearer, calmer, and easier for beginners to understand: [paste text].
Tone adjustmentRewrite this copy in a helpful, trustworthy, and practical tone without sounding salesy: [paste copy].
Offer positioningHelp me position [product/service] for [audience]. Include pain point, promise, proof, and CTA.
Case study draftCreate a simple case study template showing how [audience] can use [tool/service] to solve [problem].
Prompt improvementImprove this prompt so it gives more specific and useful results: [paste prompt].
Decision guideCreate a decision guide to help [audience] choose between [option A], [option B], and [option C].
Reflection journalCreate 7 reflection questions for creators reviewing their weekly content, tools, and business progress.
