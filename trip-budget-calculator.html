How the estimate is built

The calculator treats transport and activities as trip totals, accommodation as a nightly cost, and food as a per-person daily cost. The buffer is added after the main estimate.

Costs people often miss

Check luggage, airport transfers, local taxes, resort fees, card charges, roaming, tips, insurance, visas, parking, and exchange-rate movement. A buffer is not a substitute for travel insurance.
