Editorial policy

Useful context, honest limits, visible disclosures.

This policy applies to all Life Upgraded by Evelyn pages.

Last reviewed: 24 July 2026

Editorial identity

Evelyn is a digital editorial persona. KokoroFlow's site owner is responsible for publishing decisions and compliance.

Selection guides versus reviews

A selection guide explains criteria using public product information and general research. A review may describe first-hand experience only when a real human test occurred and the conditions, duration, free samples, and limitations are documented.

Affiliate independence

Commission eligibility does not guarantee inclusion or a positive conclusion. We may remove a recommendation if information changes, the product becomes unavailable, or the page no longer adds value.

Beauty and health wording

We avoid diagnosing conditions, promising treatment, or presenting cosmetic imagery as medical evidence. Ingredient concerns, allergies, persistent irritation, and health conditions should be discussed with a qualified professional.

Corrections

Material corrections should update the review date and, where useful, briefly explain what changed. Readers can report an issue through the contact page.

Commercial disclosure

A clear disclosure appears near an affiliate recommendation. Site-wide details are available in the affiliate disclosure.
