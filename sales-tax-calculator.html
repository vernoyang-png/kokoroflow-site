Sales Tax and VAT Calculator

Add tax to a net price or find the tax already included in a gross price.

Calculate tax

Choose the right calculation

Add tax when the amount you entered does not yet include sales tax or VAT. This is common when estimating a US checkout total from a listed pre-tax price, or when adding VAT to a business quotation.

Extract tax when the price already includes VAT and you want to see the underlying net price and tax amount. This is often useful when reviewing consumer prices in the UK or Europe.

Important: Rates and taxability vary by location, product, buyer, and transaction. Use an official tax source for an actual invoice or filing.

Worked example

If a net price is €80 and VAT is 20%, the tax is €16 and the gross price is €96. If the displayed price is €96 including 20% VAT, extracting the tax returns a net price of €80 and VAT of €16.
