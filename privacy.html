Privacy policy

Your privacy at KokoroFlow

This policy explains what information may be processed when you use kokoroflow.net.

Last updated: 23 July 2026

1. Information you provide

If you email us, we receive the information you include, such as your email address, name, and message. Please do not send sensitive personal or financial information.

2. Tool inputs

Most KokoroFlow calculators run directly in your browser. We do not intentionally collect the amounts, text, passwords, or other values you enter into these tools. Generated passwords should still be handled carefully and stored in a reputable password manager.

3. Technical and usage data

Our hosting provider may process standard server information such as IP address, browser type, device type, requested page, referring page, and the date and time of a request. This information may be used for security, reliability, troubleshooting, and aggregate traffic measurement.

4. Cookies and consent

KokoroFlow may use essential cookies needed for site operation. If analytics or advertising services are introduced, visitors in regions where consent is required will be offered appropriate choices before non-essential cookies are used.

5. Advertising and third-party services

KokoroFlow is preparing to use services such as Google AdSense, but advertising may not be active on every page or at every time. If advertising is enabled, third-party vendors may use cookies or similar technologies to serve and measure ads in accordance with their own policies and applicable consent requirements.

Links to other websites are governed by the privacy policies of those websites. We are not responsible for their content or data practices.

6. Affiliate links

Some pages may contain clearly identified affiliate links. Clicking such a link may allow the merchant or affiliate network to record a referral. See our Affiliate Disclosure.

7. Data retention and security

We retain correspondence only as long as reasonably needed to respond, maintain records, or meet legal obligations. No online service can guarantee absolute security, but we take reasonable steps to reduce unnecessary collection and protect the information we handle.

8. Your choices

Depending on where you live, you may have rights relating to access, correction, deletion, objection, or restriction of personal data. To make a privacy enquiry, email hello@kokoroflow.net. We may need to verify the request.

9. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy when services or legal requirements change. The updated date will appear at the top of this page.
