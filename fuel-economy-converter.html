Why US and Imperial MPG differ

A US gallon and an Imperial gallon are different sizes, so the same vehicle will show a different MPG figure under each system. L/100km also works in the opposite direction: a lower number means less fuel used.

Use the result as a comparison

Real fuel use changes with speed, traffic, temperature, tyres, load, terrain, and driving style. Manufacturer ratings and this conversion are useful starting points, not guarantees.

Quick reminder: higher MPG is better; lower L/100km is better.
