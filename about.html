About KokoroFlow

Useful answers should feel clear, calm, and honest.

KokoroFlow is an independent website offering free browser-based tools and plain-English guides for common decisions at home, at work, and while travelling.

What we publish

Our tools cover everyday calculations and small tasks: tips, sales tax, VAT, mortgages, savings, unit conversions, time zones, word counts, passwords, and more. Our guides explain the assumptions behind those tools so a result is easier to use responsibly.

We write primarily for English-speaking readers in the United States, United Kingdom, and Europe. Where local conventions differ, we label them clearly rather than pretending one answer fits every country.

How we design a useful page

Clear purpose

Each page should solve one recognisable problem and explain what information you need before you begin.

Visible assumptions

Estimates are only useful when rates, units, formulas, and exclusions are easy to understand.

No required account

Our core tools are designed to work without registration. Where possible, inputs stay in your browser.

Honest limitations

We identify when a tool is for general information and when a local professional or official source is more appropriate.

Editorial and commercial independence

KokoroFlow may use advertising or affiliate links. Commercial relationships do not change the purpose of a page or the calculation it provides. Any affiliate link will be labelled, and recommendations will include useful comparison criteria rather than copied product descriptions.

We do not offer personalised financial, medical, legal, or tax advice. Tools provide estimates for general information only.

Corrections and suggestions

If you spot an error, an unclear explanation, or a tool that behaves unexpectedly, please contact us. Helpful corrections are reviewed and used to improve the relevant page.
