Why the date is required

Time differences can change when regions begin or end daylight saving time on different dates. This converter uses the selected date and the browser's current time-zone database instead of assuming a fixed year-round offset.

Use the result safely

For an important meeting, include the date and reference city in the invitation, then ask participants to confirm the time displayed by their own calendar. Local rules and software time-zone data can change.
