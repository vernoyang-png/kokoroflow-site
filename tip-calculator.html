How to use this tip calculator

Enter the bill amount, choose a tip percentage, and set the number of people splitting the bill. The calculator estimates the tip, final total, and cost per person.

Common US tip ranges

  • 15% can be used for basic service.
  • 18% to 20% is common for many restaurants.
  • More than 20% may fit excellent service or larger groups.

This is only a convenience estimate. Local habits and service charges may vary.
