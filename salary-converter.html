Estimate gross pay

What this estimate includes

The annual result is hourly rate × weekly hours × paid weeks. Monthly pay is the annual figure divided by 12. It is a gross estimate before income tax, social contributions, pension deductions, benefits, unpaid leave, bonuses, or overtime premiums.

Compare jobs carefully

An employee and a contractor with the same headline hourly rate may not receive the same overall value. Compare paid leave, insurance, retirement contributions, equipment, non-billable time, and employment taxes.
