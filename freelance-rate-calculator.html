Why billable hours matter

Freelancers also spend time on proposals, administration, marketing, learning, bookkeeping, and unpaid revisions. Using total working hours instead of billable hours usually understates the required rate.

What this tool does not calculate

Taxes, pension contributions, insurance, payment delays, bad debt, platform fees, currency charges, and local legal obligations differ. Add them to business costs or seek professional advice where necessary.
